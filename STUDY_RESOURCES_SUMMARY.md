## 🎯 Most Important Takeaways

### Critical Study Focus Areas
1. **19 CFR Part 111** - Customs Brokers (MOST TESTED!)
   - § 111.23 (Responsible supervision)
   - § 111.36 (Relationships with unlicensed persons)
   - § 111.45 (Recordkeeping)

2. **General Rules of Interpretation (GRIs)**
   - All 6 rules must be mastered
   - Apply in order - don't skip ahead
   - Practice 50+ classification problems

3. **Valuation Methods (§ 152.103-107)**
   - Must be applied in order
   - Transaction value is PRIMARY method
   - Know what's included/excluded

4. **Time Limits**
   - Entry filing: 15 calendar days
   - Entry summary: 10 working days
   - Protest: 180 days
   - Recordkeeping: 5 years

### Study Strategies That Work
1. ✅ Start 3-6 months before exam
2. ✅ Take 5-10 full-length timed practice exams
3. ✅ Tab HTSUS and CFR strategically (don't over-tab!)
4. ✅ Study with 1-2 partners (not large groups)
5. ✅ Focus on WHERE to find info (not memorization)
6. ✅ Practice navigation speed with reference materials
7. ✅ Review both correct AND incorrect answers

### Common Mistakes to Avoid
1. ❌ Over-tabbing reference materials
2. ❌ Not taking enough practice exams
3. ❌ Joining large study groups
4. ❌ Procrastinating (starting too late)
5. ❌ Over-relying on memorization
6. ❌ Ignoring weak areas
7. ❌ Not reading questions carefully
8. ❌ Spending too long on one question

- **Answer easy questions first** - build momentum
